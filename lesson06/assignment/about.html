About!

El Salvadorean Cusine is heavily influenced from Aztec and Mayan hertiage as well as spanish and other european cuisines.

The national dish of El Salvador is the Pupusas made with corn meal is a thick tortilla stuff with fillings such as pork beans and cheese, these tasty treats can be found in food carts at every corner around towns.

El Salvador is located in a tropical climate and lot of tropical fruits grow there such as papaya, and tamaraind which you'll often find as juices and many restaurants.
